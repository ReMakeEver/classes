Come In For a Satisfying Drink and Show

The Tombstone Vigilantes stage mock gunfights and hangings to the delight of our many tourists. This photo shows a tourist about to be hanged for some offense in front of the Crystal Palace saloon. Don't worry, though, most of our tourists escape unharmed at the end of their visit!

Today, Allen Street is lined with small shops and art galleries. The shop keepers welcome you and are eager to share some of the old Tombstone stories.
