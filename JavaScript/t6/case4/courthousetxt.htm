Justice - One Hanging at a Time

The Tombstone courthouse was the center of justice for all of Cochise County in its early years. The building was erected in 1882 and still stands today as a testament to the days when Tombstone was much wilder. The county seat (and its court) moved many years ago, but the building has since become a State Park with a museum that contains an interesting collection of old west memorabilia.
